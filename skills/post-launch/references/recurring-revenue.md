# Recurring Revenue Reference

Every project you complete is a potential recurring revenue source. Offer these services after every delivery. The goal is predictable monthly income that does not require constantly finding new clients.

---

## 1. Monthly Maintenance Plans

The most important recurring service. Offer after every project without exception.

### Tiers

**Basic — $75/month**
- Monthly check that site is live and loading correctly
- Browser and mobile compatibility check
- Minor content updates — up to 1 hour of changes per month
- Monthly email summary

**Standard — $125/month**
- Everything in Basic
- Up to 2 hours of content changes per month
- Priority response (4 business hours vs 24)
- Quarterly 15-minute check-in call

**Priority — $200/month**
- Everything in Standard
- Up to 4 hours of changes per month
- Same-day response for urgent issues
- Annual design refresh consultation

### Pitch Script

Send 24–48 hours after project delivery:

```
Hi [Name],

How's the site feeling now that you've had a day with it?

I offer a monthly maintenance plan so you never have to worry
about your site going down or something breaking:

BASIC — $75/month
  Monthly health check, minor content updates (1 hr/month),
  browser/mobile check, monthly summary.

STANDARD — $125/month
  Above plus up to 2 hours of changes and priority response.

No long-term commitment — month to month. If you're interested,
just reply and I'll set it up.

[Your Name]
```

### Maintenance Agreement Language

> "Monthly maintenance: $[amount]/month billed on the 1st.
> Includes: [list]. Additional hours billed at $75/hour.
> Cancel with 7 days written notice."

### Time Reality

Most Basic clients take 30–60 minutes per month. If a client consistently runs 2+ hours, have a conversation about upgrading to Standard.

## 2. Hosting Reselling

Manage hosting under your Netlify account and charge clients monthly.

**Your cost:** Netlify Pro — $19/month (covers multiple sites)
**You charge:** $25–$35/month per client

**What you handle:** domain renewals, SSL, deployment, any hosting errors.
**Client experience:** they never log into a dashboard.

### Pitch Script

```
"I can handle your hosting for $25/month — your site stays live,
I deal with any technical issues, and you don't have to log in
anywhere. Domain renewal is separate (~$12/year) and I'll remind
you before it's due."
```

### Notes

- Track all clients: hosting plan status, domain renewal dates, billing dates
- Never let a client domain expire — give 60-day renewal reminders
- For Vercel-hosted sites (React/Next.js): Vercel Pro is $20/month — charge $30–$40 to cover cost + margin

## 3. Content Updates Retainer

Client pays monthly for a set amount of content changes.

**Best for:** restaurants (seasonal menus), service businesses (new offerings), anyone wanting a blog.

**Pricing:**
- Up to 2 hours/month: $100–$150/month
- Up to 4 hours/month: $175–$225/month
- Blog post (500–800 words, SEO-optimized): $75–$150 per post

### Pitch Script

```
"I offer a content retainer — you send me updates and I get them
on the site within 48 hours. $125/month for up to 2 hours of
changes. A lot easier than figuring out how to do it yourself."
```

### Scope Rules

- Define what counts as a content change vs a new feature (new feature = change order)
- Specify turnaround (48 hours standard)
- Unused hours do not roll over

## 4. SEO Retainer

Only offer this when genuinely comfortable with seo-basics. Do not sell what you cannot deliver.

**Starter SEO retainer: $150–$250/month**

Includes:
- Monthly Google Business Profile post
- Draft responses for new reviews (client approves)
- One 500–700 word blog post targeting a local keyword
- Monthly rankings check for 3–5 target keywords
- One-page monthly report

**Best candidates:** plumbers, electricians, HVAC, restaurants, home services — any business where local Google search drives phone calls.

**Pitch:**
```
"Staying active on Google makes a big difference for local businesses.
I offer a basic local SEO package for $200/month — monthly GBP post,
one blog post targeting a local search term, and a simple rankings
report. Want me to show you what that would look like for [Business]?"
```

**Set realistic expectations:** SEO takes 3–6 months to show meaningful results. Results depend on competition. This is a starting point, not a full campaign.

## The Business Math

| What you have | Monthly recurring |
|---|---|
| 5 maintenance clients at $75 | $375 |
| 10 maintenance clients at $75 | $750 |
| 10 maintenance + 3 hosting ($30) | $840 |
| Above + 2 content retainers ($125) | $1,090 |

At 10 maintenance + 3 hosting + 2 content retainers, you generate $1,090/month before landing any new project. That is the buffer that eliminates slow-month panic. Build toward it deliberately — every delivered project is an opportunity to add one more recurring client.

## Monthly Maintenance Report Template

The single biggest driver of retainer retention is making the value visible. A client paying $75/month who never hears from you starts wondering what they're paying for, and cancels. A client who gets a short, clear report every month sees the value and renews without thinking about it. Send this on the same day each month (e.g. the 1st), even in months when nothing was requested — *especially* those months.

Keep it to one screen. The goal is reassurance, not a wall of text.

```
Subject: [Business Name] — Site Report, [Month Year]

Hi [Name],

Quick monthly check-in on your site. Everything's running well —
here's the rundown.

SITE HEALTH
- Uptime: [99.9% / no outages this month]
- Performance: [PageSpeed mobile score, or "fast, no issues"]
- Backups: [current — last backup [date]]
- Security & updates: [SSL valid; plugins/dependencies up to date]

WHAT I DID THIS MONTH
- [e.g. Updated holiday hours on the homepage and contact page]
- [e.g. Swapped in 3 new photos on the gallery]
- [e.g. Fixed contact-form spacing on mobile]
- [If nothing was requested: "No changes requested — ran the monthly
   check and everything's healthy."]

RECOMMENDATION (optional)
- [One small, genuine suggestion — e.g. "Your reviews section is a bit
   thin; adding 2–3 recent Google reviews would help conversions. Want
   me to handle it next month?"]

You're all set. Reply any time you want something changed — it's
included in your plan.

[Your Name]
[your phone]
```

**Why this works:** it proves the site is cared for even in quiet months (no-contact months are the most common cancellation trigger), and the optional recommendation is a low-key, non-pushy path into a content retainer or a one-off upsell. For STANDARD, SEO, or higher tiers, add a short metrics line — traffic, search impressions, or keyword movement — so the higher price stays visibly justified.
